Breweries in North America and Hawaii

Authored by: Aimee Armour-Avant

Aztec Aquaponics, a small company based out of Albuquerque, specializes in using beer brewing byproducts for their aquaponic operations. They produce sustainable fish and lettuce greens that supply an artisan fast-casual national restaurant chain.

Aztec Aquaponics need to find high-density areas of breweries to maximize their aquaponic production. Their supply chain requires that they cannot transport the brewery byproducts more than 25 km. Where should they locate?

Hex binning brewery locations in North America and Hawaii offers a potential solution.

Point locations of breweries in GeoJSON format: link to the GeoJSON

Recommendation: Aztec Aquaponics should open a branch in Austin, Texas.
