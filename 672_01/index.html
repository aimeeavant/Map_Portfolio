Edinburgh, Scotland

About this Map and Webpage

This map is made using the Leaflet web mapping javascript library. The basemap is provided by Open Topo and uses data from Open Street Map.

The map is centered on Edinburgh, Scotland and zoomed to a level of 7 to provide context to the topograpy around the city. The map can be scrolled in to reveal more detail or out to show further context. The image of Edinburgh at the right can be clicked to reveal another image.
